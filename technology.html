BE AGILE AND KEEP LEAN WITH OUR PROCESS

We created CODE to help you better understand what we do and how we can help you optimize your business with software, but it doesn’t mean we do it without control!

COMMUNICATE WITH SLACK

Working with brothers means you can talk to us like brothers. You will be part of our team on Slack so you can ask questions and view all project communication on our side.

TAKE CONTROL ON PROJECT WITH JIRA OR TRELLO

We like to keep it Lean rather than Waterfall because it’s faster and better. But we need the right software to manage all projects. We use Jira or Trello to make sure you have all the knowledge you need..

PAY FOR WORKED HOURS

The only way to stop wasting your and our time is to pay only for worked hours.

KEEP IT SIMPLE!

We are your brothers and we are here for you. Rememeber that. So if you want to use your own way of communication and project management, just tell us about it!

Process steps in our projects

  • Idea

    Requirement Gathering , Analysis and with the requirements Design an architecture of the project and the Tech Stack to be implemented.

  • Design

    HLD , LLD design are done during this phase for the project and any Technical Decisions should be taken here.

  • Development

    Start of Coding , Unit Testing of the compenents and they are integrated and passed on for QA Testing and Defect Solving continues till the Project is ready for release

  • Deployment

    Deployment of the project in cloud or VM's or in Web Hosting Server to make the Project Live and available for use for the customers.
